Our Philosophy

Where vintage
meets obsession.

Kevin Moll opened Skylabs Audio in 2016 with one mission: to bring the golden era of hi-fi back to life. Located in the historic Valley Junction district of West Des Moines, we don't just sell equipment — we curate experiences. Every turntable, every amplifier, every speaker system is chosen because we'd put it in our own listening room.
